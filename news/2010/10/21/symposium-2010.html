Second Annual SRL/ISSDM Research Symposium and UCSC Systems Oktoberfest

SRL Team Oct 21, 2010

The two-day Symposium featured presentations from SRL and ISSDM students; keynote addresses by UCSC alumni / New Dream Founder Sage Weil and LANL Division leader Gary Grider; and a delicious Oktoberfest Barbeque dinner. Attendees included industry partners from Data Domain, EMC, Hitachi Data Systems, IBM, Microsoft, NetApp, Panasas and Parascale. Attendees from the National Labs represented Los Alamos, Lawrence Livermore and Lawrence Berkeley Labs.
