Esteban Molina Estolano


Email: eestolan@cs.ucsc.edu

Esteban Molina-Estolano received a Master’s degree in Computer Science in 2011.
He worked on scalable simulation of parallel filesystems with Carlos Maltzahn, in collaboration with Los Alamos National Laboratory. Before coming to UCSC, he completed a B.S. in Computer Science at Harvey Mudd College.
